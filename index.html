Monolith Lapidary - Our Story

Monolith Lapidary has been an idea in the mind of our head lapidarist, Tom Strong since 2018. In that time - and for years before - he has been honing his craft while continuing to learn the subtle nuance of each gemstone. As the finished gemstones began piling up and relatives and friends became nauseated at the sheer number of cabochons, pendants, and jewelry, necessity prescribed an outlet. This is that outlet. Through Monolith Lapidary you can get the best that Tom has to offer. When designing cabochons Tom compromises his vision with the stone itself, listening to and feeling out the flow and feel of each unique stone. Each designer cabochon is unique in shape, size, and function. You will smile each time you are complimented on your perfect, unique stone.

The Mission

We take pride in providing only the best stones, with the highest polish. All of our gemstones are meticulously scanned for debilitating defects, flaws, or slight imperfections. We can promise a lasting shine for all your gemstones.

"We strive to bring a shine to your world and a light to your eye.

We. Are. Monolith."
